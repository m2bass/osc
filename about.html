Our Purpose

Our purpose is to offer valuable services by providing information, resources, activities and programs to engage, educate and help support the needs of the community, enriching our neighborhoods and its residents.

The Mission of Ophelia Steen Family & Health Services Center is to provide vital care and services to residents in the City of Hammond and surrounding neighborhoods.
